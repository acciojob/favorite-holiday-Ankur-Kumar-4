Maldives

The Maldives , officially the Republic of Maldives It lies southwest of Sri Lanka and India, about 750 kilometres (470 miles; 400 nautical miles) from the Asian continent's mainland. The Maldives' chain of 26 atolls stretches across the equator from Ihavandhippolhu Atol l in the north to Addu Atoll in the south.
